1) Attach the PlanarReflectionManager class to your main camera object
2) Associate your floor object with the ReflectionPlane variable
3) Associate the the provided FloorMaterial material with the FloorMaterial variable
4) Assign an appropriate base texture to the FloorStencil material (an example is provided for you)
5) Assign the FloorStencil material to your floor object
6) Press play!
